About Bismi.

Bismi Cultural Centre is a charitable organization located near Calicut Airport. Bismi helps families who are suffering in society, based on Holy Quran and Prophet's Behaviour. This organization started in 1995 by providing food, education, and medical care to the three families. Nowadays, we can provide relief to many families suffering in various ways with the blessings of Allah. With the help of generous donors, the organization works by considering the problems of the people facing difficulties regardless of caste and religion.

How You Can Help?.

You can participate physically and financially in various activities of the Bismi Cultural Centre.

Our Activities.

Orphane Care

Orphans who have lost their father's support are kept in their homes with their mothers and provided with the necessary study materials, food, clothes, etc.

Physiotherapy Centre

The Physiotherapy Center operates six days a week with the help of modern equipment led by experienced Physiotherapists and Doctors.

Ramzan Kit Distribution

All the deserving families are provided with the necessary resources to fast during the holy month of Ramadan, delivered to their homes a week in advance.

Food Kit Distribution

Delivering essential food to orphans and the poor who are suffering even for an early meal.

Psychiatric Clinic

Mentally disturbed persons are treated at the organization on Saturdays under the guidance of psychiatrist doctors.

General Clinic & Pharmacy

Bismi provides a Medical clinic under a civil surgeon on Sundays. Hundreds of patients use this service on Sundays.

Educare

Bags, uniforms, and other materials are distributed to more than a thousand needy students from deserving families.

Rehab Aid Centre

Rehab Aid Centre - Water beds, air beds, wheelchairs, walkers, commode chairs, structures, oxygen cylinders, and hospital coats.

Home for Homeless

Home for Homeless - Create new houses or repair the existing ones for those who are deserving.

Cloth Collection

Cloth Collection - Collect the new and out-of-fashion items and distribute them to the needy.

Self Employment Scheme

The Organization conducts self-employment training to make families and persons with disabilities self-sufficient.

Rehabilitation Centre

A rehabilitation center is a place for people who are starving and living in rented quarters as a relief until they have their own homes and facilities.

Fresh Water

Wells and tubewells are constructed to relieve the people travel long distances for water.
